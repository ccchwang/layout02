Web aficionado since 1999 and part-time Mayor of Falls Church.

Viget’s original engineering + design combo. An engineering major and studio art minor was rare at UVA in the mid-90s. This mix became Brian’s career focus when he co-founded Viget to bring together the technical and design talent that clients needed.


Brian does everything from leading company direction to advising key clients to meeting every potential hire to making our conference tables with his bare hands. A focused man of many talents or a distracted man of many interests? We may never know.
